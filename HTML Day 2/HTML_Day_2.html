Program Overview

The MSIT program is designed to equip students with skills and knowledge needed to excel in the field of information technology. Our curriculum is tailored to meet the needs of both beginners and experienced professionals.

Curriculum

The curriculum covers a wide range of topics, including:

  • Data Structure and Algorithms
  • Database Management Systems
  • Web Development
  • Cloud Computing
  • Cybersecurity

Admission Requirements

To apply for the MSIT program, you will need:

  • A bachelor's degree in a related field
  • Official transcripts
  • Resume/CV
  • Statement of purpose
  • Two letters of recommendation
